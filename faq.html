We love to answer your questions.

What are the basics?

A idea is a finite work with a clear goal that you’d like to bring to life. Think albums, books, or films.
The funding goal is the amount of money that a entrepreneur needs to complete their idea.
A entrepreneur is the person or team behind the idea idea, working to bring it to life.
Investor are folks who pledge money to join entrepreneurs in bringing ideas to life.
Here entrepreneur have to share a piece of their idea with their invesot community. Typically, these are one-of-a-kind experiences, limited editions, or copies of the creative work being produced.


What is Inventors for?

Inventors is a just platform where entrepreneur can annouce his idea with investors to help him making that idea happen.


Why do people invest in entrepreneur's ideas?

Investing a idea is more than just pledging funds to a entrepreneur. It's pledging your support to a creative idea that you want to see exist in the world.


Where do investor come from?

The majority of investor always keep seeking a place to invest to get more money from that place, here they can find new ideas with Inventors.


Who is responsible for completing a idea as promised?

It's the idea entrepreneur's responsibility to complete their idea. Inventors is not involved in the development of the ideas themselves.
Inventors does not guarantee ideas or investigate a entrepreneur's ability to complete their idea. On Inventors, only investor ultimately decide wether he have to invest in that idea or not.


How do I know a idea entrepreneur is who they claim they are?

Perhaps you are friends with the entrepreneur, or you heard about the idea from a trusted source.
Looking for more information? Visit entrepreneur to get a better sense of the person or team behind the idea.
In the entrepreneur bio section of each idea, you can find additional resources, like links to websites, relevant background information, and in some cases, a connected Facebook account.
You can also send direct message to entrepreneur to get more trusted information.

Can Inventors refund the money if a idea is unable to fulfill?
No, Inventors doesn't issue refunds. Transactions are between investor and entrepreneurs directly.
SUGGETION:Investor can issue limited funds to entrepreneur to just start idea and keep him in contact every moment.
