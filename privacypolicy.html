Privacy Policy.

What We Collect?

In order for you to create an account on Inventors and use our services, we need to collect and process some information. Depending on your use of the Services, that may include:
  • information (such as your name, email and postal addresses, telephone number, and country of residence) that you provide by completing forms on Inventors, including if you register as a user of the Services, subscribe to our newsletters, upload or submit any material through Inventors, or request any information;
  • your login and password details, in connection with the account sign-in process;
  • information about your activity on and interaction with Inventors, such as your IP address and the type of device or browser you use;
  • communications you send to us (for example, when you ask for support, send us questions or comments, or report a problem); and
  • nformation that you submit to Inventors in the form of comments, contributions to discussions, or messages to other users.

What’s Private?

This data will not be publicly displayed or revealed to other users:
  • your password details
  • your IP address
  • your phone number
  • communications you send to us (for example, when you ask for support, send us questions or comments, or report a problem)
We don’t give your personal information to any third-party services, except when it’s necessary to provide Inventors’s Services (like when we partner with payment processors). When we share data with third-party services that support our delivery of the Inventors Services, we require that they protect your personal information to the same standards we do.
We do reserve the right to disclose personal information when we believe that doing so is reasonably necessary to comply with the law or law enforcement, to prevent fraud or abuse, or to protect Inventors’s legal rights.

What’s Public?

When you create an account, we create a basic profile page for you on Inventors, containing your account name, the date the account was created, and a list of projects you have backed or launched. Whenever your account name appears on Inventors (for instance, when you post comments, send messages, or back projects), people can click your account name to see your profile. The profile is not searchable on Inventors or via search engines like Google. Here are some of the things that will be publicly viewable on your profile, or elsewhere on Inventors:
  • the account name, and the date it was created
  • ny information you choose to add to your profile (like a picture, bio, or your location)
  • projects you've
  • any comments you’ve posted on Inventors
  • if you have "Liked" a project update
entrepreneurs will be asked to verify their identities before launching a project. Once this has been done, the entrepreneur’s Verified Name will be publicly displayed on their account profile and on any projects they launch.

Uses of Your Personal Information

We will use the personal information you provide to:
  • identify you when you sign in to your account;
  • enable us to provide you with the Services;
  • administer your account with us;
  • enable us to contact you regarding any question you make through the Services;
  • eanalyze the use of the Services and the people visiting to improve our content and Services; and
  • use for other purposes that we may disclose to you when we request your information.
We take securing your data and preserving your privacy very seriously. We never post anything to your Facebook, Twitter, or other third-party accounts without your permission. Inventors Project entrepreneurs never receive investors’ credit card details or other payment information. We do not and will not sell your data.

Email

We want to communicate with you only if you want to hear from us. We try to keep emails to a minimum and give you the ability to opt out of any marketing communications we send.
We may send you service-related announcements on the rare occasions when it is necessary to do so.
